        How the footprint is computed

        Given a base model, we build the lineage DAG (parent → child) and take the base plus all descendants as the family. Each model reports only its incremental training delta; the family total sums the subtree with deduplication for merged nodes. Coverage shows what fraction disclosed a dia_report footprint block — totals are a lower bound when reports are missing.

        Publisher pretraining estimates (e.g. Meta GHG tables) appear separately and are not included in DIA rollup totals unless ingested as a dia_report.
